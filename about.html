About Teknikar Plumbing

Our Story

Teknikar Plumbing was founded in 2009 by John Teknikar with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable plumbing services to our local community. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted team of plumbing professionals serving hundreds of satisfied customers each year.

John's vision was clear from day one - treat every customer like family, do the job right the first time, and never compromise on quality. This philosophy continues to guide everything we do at Teknikar Plumbing.

Over the past 15+ years, we've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time. We're proud to be the plumbing company that neighbors recommend to friends and family.
