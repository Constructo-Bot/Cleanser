Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

We aim to respond to emergency calls within 30 minutes, with technicians arriving typically within 60-90 minutes depending on your location and traffic conditions.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for most plumbing services. Emergency repairs may require immediate assessment, but we always provide pricing before proceeding with work.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed as master plumbers, carry comprehensive liability insurance, and maintain all necessary certifications for gas line work and other specialized services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. We also offer financing options for larger projects like complete repiping or major installations.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Labor warranties typically range from 1-5 years depending on the service, and we honor all manufacturer warranties on parts and equipment.
